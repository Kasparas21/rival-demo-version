# Rival — Demo Guide

## What is Rival?

Rival is an AI-powered tool that helps you see what your competitors are doing with their ads. You can look across major ad networks including Facebook, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Microsoft. It’s built for people who do marketing, growth, or research and want a clearer picture of how other brands show up in paid media—without doing everything by hand.

## What works in this demo

This is an early-stage build. The features below are live—everything else is still being developed.

**✅ What works right now:**

- Browsing the marketing site and opening the app **without** creating an account
- The **Spy** step: name a company or brand to research
- A short **“searching”** step while the app fetches ad data
- A **competitor** view: scroll through a library of real ads the tool has found
- A **Strategy overview** section with AI-style commentary on the ads
- A yellow **Demo** banner that reminds you the product is in preview

**🚧 Still in progress / not working yet:**

- Anything not listed above (this build is a slice of the full vision)
- Saving your favourite brands or searches in the long term **without** signing in (saving to your profile needs a free account)
- Full polish, extra platforms, and product features you might see on the public website roadmap

## How to try the demo (no account needed)

1. **Start** on the Rival home page.
2. Click **“Go to Demo.”** (You can ignore sign-up for this walkthrough.)
3. You’ll land on the **Spy** page. Type a company you want to learn about—use a name people would recognize (see the example below).
4. **Run the search.** The app will take you to a **searching** screen while it pulls live data. That’s normal.
5. When results are ready, you’ll go to the **competitor** view: a grid of ads and details you can click through.
6. **Scroll to the strategy section** to read a short AI-style analysis of the patterns in those ads.

**Try this if you want a ready-made example:** search for **Nike** or **Apple**—big brands usually return plenty of ad examples to explore.

## What you’ll see

- **Spy / search** — This is where you type the brand and start. Keep it to real company or product names; odd spellings or tiny brands may return little or nothing.
- **Searching / loading** — A “please wait” style step while data is fetched. First time can feel slower; that’s expected.
- **Competitor results (ads library)** — The main “wow” view: the ads themselves, with ways to open and inspect them. Think of it as a window into what that competitor is running.
- **Strategy overview (AI analysis)** — A short written summary of themes and takeaways from the ad set. It’s meant to help you see the story in the noise.

## Features that need a free account

If you want to **save** searches or keep a list of **saved competitors** tied to *you*, you’ll need a **free account** (sign up from the login page when you’re ready). A yellow **Demo** banner in the app explains that not everything is available in anonymous demo mode. You can still explore without signing in.

## Tips for the best demo experience

- **Use a real, well-known brand name** for searches (e.g. Nike, Apple) so the tool has a rich set of ads to show.
- The **first search** after opening the demo may take **roughly 20–30 seconds** while live data is fetched—grab a coffee moment, then enjoy the results.
- **Use a desktop browser** and a good-sized window so the ad grid and text are easy to read.

Enjoy the demo—and thanks for taking Rival for a spin.
